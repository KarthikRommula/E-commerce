About Us

Vaarahi – The Next Generation Farming Limited is committed to transforming *waste into wealth* by innovating sustainable solutions for modern agriculture.

Our startup specializes in *manufacturing agarbatti-making machines, producing and selling Eco friendly agarbattis, and developing **advanced farming tools* tailored for next-generation agricultural practices.

By integrating technology with sustainability, Vaarahi empowers farmers and entrepreneurs with efficient, eco-friendly solutions that enhance productivity and create new economic opportunities.
